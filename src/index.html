Designs. Displays. Props. I love 'em!

I design a variety of objects and make them with a variety materials for a variety of people. Whether it be private clients, well known brand names or commercial businesses. Imagining and manufacturing objects, varying in size and purpose, whatever the brief, I endeavor to create beautifully simple work within which form and function are equal entities. I have also worked as part of larger teams creating some pretty phenomenal installations. These include Vevo, NME and Virgin Trains. My work has previously been recognized and supported by brands such as Anglepoise and Kvadrat fabrics. My creations have also featured at music events and marketing campaigns across the UK.

If you would like to know more about what I can do for you, feel free to send me an email or invite me out for coffee... I would love to talk!
